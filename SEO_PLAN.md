# Polyglot AI Academy - SEO Plan

## 1. SEO goals

- Build organic acquisition for language learners and enterprise training buyers.
- Support public pages with SSR/SSG, metadata, JSON-LD, sitemap, robots and canonical URLs.
- Keep enterprise pages trust-oriented and conversion-ready.

## 2. Public pages

- Landing page.
- Language pages: English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean.
- Enterprise training pages: customer support, hospitality, sales, cross-border workforce, schools.
- Pricing.
- AI speaking demo.
- Blog.
- Testimonials.
- FAQ.
- Privacy, Terms, AI Safety, Data Sources.

## 3. Metadata and schema

Required:

- title.
- description.
- canonical.
- OpenGraph.
- Twitter card.
- JSON-LD where applicable:
  - Organization.
  - SoftwareApplication.
  - Course.
  - FAQ.
  - Article.
  - Breadcrumb.

## 4. Content clusters

Learner:

- English learning guide.
- Chinese HSK guide.
- Japanese JLPT guide.
- Korean TOPIK guide.
- Pronunciation tips.
- AI speaking practice.
- Grammar explainers.
- Vocabulary by topic.

Enterprise:

- AI speaking practice for customer support.
- Language training analytics for L&D.
- Hospitality English roleplay training.
- Cross-border workforce language training.
- AI language training vs traditional apps.
- Speaking practice AI vs 1:1 teacher.

## 5. Technical SEO

- SSG/SSR public pages.
- Clean URLs.
- Internal linking.
- Sitemap.
- robots.txt.
- Image optimization.
- Core Web Vitals.
- Accessible headings.
- No index for authenticated app/admin pages.

## 6. SEO Done Criteria

- Public pages have metadata and canonical.
- JSON-LD exists for relevant page types.
- Blog strategy includes learner and enterprise clusters.
- App/admin routes are not indexed.
